Visual Scrolling Tool

This Web Gazer Chrome Extension offers a powerful and accessible solution for individuals with physical disabilities, enabling them to interact with web content using only their eyes. By leveraging HTML, JavaScript, and WebGazer.js, we were able to create an intuitive, hands-free browsing experience that not only serves the disabled community but also enhances web interactions for the general public. The project demonstrates the potential of eye-tracking technology in breaking down barriers to digital accessibility and offers a glimpse into the future of more inclusive web experiences. Through careful design and thoughtful consideration of user needs, we have developed a tool that empowers users and enhances their ability to engage with digital content in a meaningful way.
